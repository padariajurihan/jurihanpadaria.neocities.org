Profile

Origin: TLOZ: Twilight Princess, TLOZ: Breath of the Wild, TLOZ: Tears of the Kingdom, TOLZ: Ocarina of Time, and many more...

Age: Unknown

Height: Unknown

Race: Hylian

Occupation: Princess of the Kingdom of Hyrule, Matriarch (Twilight Princess), Sage (Ocarina of Time), Sage of Time (Tears of the Kingdom), Scholar (Breath of the Wild), Mortal Goddess, Warrior (As Sheik), Priestess.

Personality: Her persona varies across her many incarnations, ranging from a stoic ruler to a spirited adventurer.

Tears of the Kingdom: Anxious, determined, purposeful, protective and self-sacrificing.

Breath of the Wild: Scholarly, stressed, dedicated, curious and resilient.

Twilight Princess: Regal, stoic, matriarchal, wise, selfless.

Ocarina of Time: Prophetic, proactive, guilt-ridden, stoic and strategic.

Appearance: Young Hylian girl with a slender, light-skinned body. Usually light blonde hair, though it was red in early official artwork and is a darker shade in Twilight Princess. In Tears of the Kingdom, she is depicted with a short, bob-length hair for the first time. Her eyes are traditionally blue, but depicted as green in the "Wild" timeline (Breath of the Wild and Tears of the Kingdom).

She uses a long, medieval-style dress, most often in tones of pink, but sometimes white, blue, or violet. These dresses frequently feature the Hylian Crest. She is almost always seen wearing a tiara (typically set with a red, pink, or blue jewel) and golden jewelry. Her outfits are often accompanied by long gloves, capes, or hooded cloaks.

Notable Traits: Divine Wisdom, Supernatural Heritage, Selfless Sacrifice, Adaptive Resourcefulness and Scholarly Curiosity.

Story

> Ocarina of Time: The Seventh Sage and Architect of Destiny

In this version, Zelda is the "Princess of Destiny" who attempts to prevent Ganondorf's rise after having prophetic visions in her dreams. After the villain takes over Hyrule, she assumes the identity of Sheik, disguising herself as a young Sheikah warrior to guide Link and avoid capture while awaiting the hero's return. As the seventh Sage and leader of all other sages, she is essential in sealing Ganon in the Sacred Realm. At the end, she decides to send Link back to his childhood to regain his lost years, an act that ultimately splits the series' timeline

> Twilight Princess: The Stoic and Sacrificed Ruler

Zelda is portrayed as the matriarch and sovereign ruler of Hyrule who is forced to surrender to Zant to prevent the slaughter of her people. Unlike other inhabitants, she remains in physical form even under the influence of the Twilight, possibly protected by the Triforce of Wisdom. She demonstrates immense altruism by sacrificing her own life essence to heal Midna, who was on the verge of death. In the final battle, after her body is briefly possessed by Ganondorf, she fights alongside Link on horseback, using Light Arrows to weaken the King of Evil.

> Breath of the Wild: The Scholar and Guardian of the Seal

Breath of the Wild: The Scholar and Guardian of the Seal In this timeline, Zelda is a princess with an inclination for science and academic research, dedicating herself to the study of ancient technology such as Guardians and Divine Beasts. She suffers for years under the pressure from her father and the kingdom to awaken her sacred sealing powers, which she only manages to do at the last moment to save Link's life. After the fall of Hyrule, she spends 100 years in a battle of wills inside the castle, keeping Calamity Ganon contained until Link awakens from his regenerative sleep.

Tears of the Kingdom: The Sage of Time and the Light Dragon

Zelda takes on a role of eternal sacrifice when she is transported to the remote past, in the era of Hyrule's foundation, where she becomes the Sage of Time and learns from the first monarchs, Rauru and Sonia. Upon discovering that the Master Sword was damaged, she decides to undergo the forbidden act of draconification, ingesting her secret stone to become the Light Dragon. In this immortal form without human consciousness, she flies over the skies of Hyrule for millennia, bathing the sacred sword in her light to restore the blade's power and deliver it to Link in the future
