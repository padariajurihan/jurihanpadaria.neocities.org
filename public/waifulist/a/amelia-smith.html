Story

> A brief overview of the true Assault Spy

A female agent of the international intelligence organization, the CIA. Despite being talented in combat, she's terrible at espionage. She is constantly at full throttle, and tries to solve every problem by punching it. For that reason, people call her the "Assault Spy" behind her back. She has a day job as a waitress.p>
